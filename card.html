The Pufferfish

Just look at his little face! Surely he couldn’t do any harm…? Don’t count on it! The pufferfish is in fact the second most poisonous vertebrate on the planet.

  • Scientific Name: Tetraodontidae
  • Life span: 4 to 8 years
  • Habitat: Ocean
  • Diet: Omnivore

Their venon kills by paralysing the diaphragm and has no known antidote. Pufferfish can inflate into a ball shape to evade predators. Also known as blowfish, these clumsy swimmers fill their elastic stomachs with huge amounts of water (and sometimes air) and blow themselves up to several times their normal size.
